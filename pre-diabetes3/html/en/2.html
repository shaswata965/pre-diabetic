Why is healthy eating important?

There is no substitute for eating a healthy and well-balanced diet. In this lesson, we’ll take you through the basics of how to eat healthy. We’ll make it easy by focusing on how to assemble a healthy plate. Before we get started, here are a few reasons why healthy eating is important:

  • Attain and maintain a healthy body weight

  • Provide adequate nutrition to your body

  • Feel good and have more energy

  • Prevent or delay diabetes related to acute and chronic complications

  • Keep your blood glucose levels, blood pressure, and cholesterol in your target ranges
