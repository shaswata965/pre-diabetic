Watch out for sugar

Having too much sugar in your diet can cause obesity, heart disease and diabetes. It’s important that you restrict your consumption of added sugars, which are simple carbs.

Many of the processed foods found at your nearest grocery store, even the savory ones, have sugar added to them. These sugars are added by the manufacturer, cook or consumer. You may be consuming more sugar from the box of biscuits that you’re snacking on than the sugar you add to your tea or coffee.

You may not always know if an ingredient is a sugar, so check food labels and watch out for these common sugars:
