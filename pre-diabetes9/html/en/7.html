Step 4: Make a Positive Action Plan

Make a positive action plan.
Mouna picks the option to prepare breakfast the night before, her action plan could look like this:

I will…
Prepare breakfast the night before

When?
Sunday and Tuesday of every week

I will do this first...
Shop for breakfast ingredients

Roadblocks
If I forget to pack breakfast the night before, I will:

Make sure I have fruit i can grab for the go

Order a healthy salad at work for an early lunch

I will do this to make my success more likely…
Have breakfast with a family member at home so i’m held accountable
