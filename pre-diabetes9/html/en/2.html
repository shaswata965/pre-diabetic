Why are these steps important?

The problems in life can be overwhelming at times. Following these steps can help you better manage any problems you face by being more thoughtful in our search for solutions. It can help you break down large problems into smaller ones. It can help you be clear about what exactly the problem is and what the options are.

Try this exercise: think about a problem or barrier you are facing right now that is preventing you from reaching your health goals. Keep this problem in your head while you read the lesson to see how you can apply what you learn.
