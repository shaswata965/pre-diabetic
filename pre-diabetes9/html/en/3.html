Step-by-Step Problem Solving

Follow this 5-step plan so you can address your problems head on!

  • Describe the problem in detail. Don’t be scared to get specific. What is it exactly that is causing this problem and how is it affecting your life.

  • Brainstorm your options. This is the time to get creative. Think about possible solutions you can do solve your problem or prevent it from happening again

  • Choose one option to try. When picking an option, think about the pros and cons.

  • Make a positive action plan. Your plan should address the following:

    1. I will…
    2. When…
    3. I will do this first…
    4. My roadblocks are…
    5. I will do this to make my success more likely…

  • Action! After executing your plan, think to yourself: Did it work? If not, what went wrong? What can I do to make this a better plan? What other plan should I try?
