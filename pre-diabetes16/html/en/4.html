How to keep it off!

However, it is possible to keep the weight off, as long as you know how. Studies show that the longer someone stays at their healthy goal weight, the more likely their body will embrace the new weight. There are a few things you should focus on when it comes to keeping the weight off.

  1. Exercise. Exercise plays a very important role in keeping the weight off. Exercise, especially strength training, can help increase your metabolism.
  2. Healthy Eating Habits. These past few months have not been about a quick fix, but about developing habits for a healthier lifestyle. This is the time to make sure the skills you have learned throughout the program, such as responding to unhealthy food triggers, into habits. Your healthy eating habits may not be exactly the same as other people’s and that’s okay. However, research shows that there are common habits practiced among people who have kept a 30 lb weight loss for at least a year. These habits include:
    1. Eating breakfast
    2. Weighing themselves at least once a week
    3. Watching less than 10 hours of TV per week
    4. Exercise at least one hour everyday, on average
