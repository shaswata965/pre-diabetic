So what do you do if you slip up?

Unhealthy fats like saturated and trans fats have been shown to increase your risk for heart disease if consumed too much. You’re better off sticking with the healthy kinds of fats, like monounsaturated fats, polyunsaturated fats and Omega-3 fatty acids... but you should still limit your health fats as they are high in calories.How we react to our slip ups can become a habit. It’s important to make sure that you make it a good habit to get you back on track.

  • Replace negative thoughts with positive ones
  • Get curious; ask yourself why you did what you did
  • Regain control as soon as you can; prepare yourself a healthy meal or schedule your next workout
  • Get support from someone close to you
  • Focus on all the progress you have already made
  • Think about how you can avoid this in the future
