Laila's Story

After having two kids, Laila struggled to lose the extra weight, which placed her at risk for type 2 diabetes. Her doctor recommends Droobi. Laila starts eating a healthier diet and checking her portion sizes. She commits to eating breakfast everyday to avoid overeating throughout the day. When her kids have football practice, she fast walks around the field they play on to burn calories while watching them play. After four months, Laila has successfully reduced her risk by reaching her target weight and activity goal.

Soon after, Laila no longer feels motivated to continue with her healthy habits. She starts skipping breakfast and overeating during lunch. At practices, Laila now sits to chat with other parents instead of her usual exercise. She is worried that if she continues she will regain all the weight back.
