Stay Motivated

When you first started losing weight, the reward of losing weight alone was likely very motivating; you felt lighter, better and healthier. But how do you continue to stay motivated if you have hit a weight loss plateau? Here are a few ways to stay motivated:

  • No healthy choicesKeep track of the goals you have already reached and think about the new ones you want to achieve.

  • Recognize your successes.

  • Keep visible signs of your progress.

  • Keep track of your weight, eating, and activity. Chat with your coach for help.

  • Add variety to your routine.

  • Identify new ways to challenge and reward yourself.

  • Create some friendly competition by challenging a friend to achieve a higher number of steps for the week.

  • Remember that slip-ups are normal and happen to everyone.

  • Look to others for help in staying motivated.
