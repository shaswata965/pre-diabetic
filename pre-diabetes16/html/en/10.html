How to Cope with Challenges

It’s not always easy to stay motivated. Here are some ways to deal with common challenges that prevent people from sustaining change.

“I’m bored.”

  • Set new goals for yourself.
  • Take part in a friendly competition.
  • Try new recipes and physical activities.
  • Work out while you talk on the phone.
  • Work out while you watch TV or videos.
  • Work out with a friend or family member

“I have already reached my weight goal.”

  • Remind yourself of the other benefits of eating right and being active.
  • Eating well and exercise makes you feel good from the inside and out. Change your perspective so that you how you feeling healthy and energized is more important than just seeing a number on the weight scale.

“I’ve gotten off track.”

  • Stay positive.
  • Follow the five steps of problem solving:
    1. Describe your problems
    2. Come up with options
    3. Choose the best options.
    4. Make an action plan.
    5. Try it

“My weight loss has slowed down.”

  • Build muscle
  • Burn more calories.
  • Take in fewer calories: portion sizes are usually bigger during plateaus
  • Continue to track what you eat and drink:take control of your behavior by becoming more mindfu

“I’ve made less progress than I expected to.”

  • Celebrate your successes.
  • Look at how far you’ve come.
  • Make a new action plan.
  • Make sure you are tracking the right way
