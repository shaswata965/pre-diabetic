How to Deal with Slip-Ups and Prevent Relapse

To begin, you have to fully address your small lapses by following the process below:

  • Realize that everyone makes mistakes now and again.

  • Resist the urge to think negatively about yourself and feel overly guilty. Slipping up doesn’t make you a failure, it only means that you are human!

  • Explore what happened. Get curious and see what triggered you to do the bad unhealthy behavior.

  • Regain control by getting back on track as soon as possible. You can’t spend time feeling guilty if you’re already back to your better habits.

  • Get support from people who are important to you

  • Remember you are making lifestyle changes and not just going for a quick fix. Life's a journey filled with ups and downs. Focus on the great progress you made and move forward!
