Let’s Revisit Laila’s Story

Let’s revisit Leila’s story... After realizing that she has gone off track, Leila wants to take action before she starts to gain weight. Leila decides to start following her old healthy eating plan, not just for her own benefit, but for her children and family. She wants to be an example for her kids to follow so they can grow up with their own healthy habits. She finds that this provides her with stronger, longer-lasting motivation. Laila also seeks out support by having her husband eat healthy with her, so they can encourage each other. At football practice, her friends have joined her to fast walk.

If slip ups are not kept in check, a person may begin to relapse. Relapse occurs when you return to practicing older habits like overeating and not being active regularly. Relapses occur over time and the best way to prevent them is to address each slip up fully as they happen. A sign that you have relapsed is when you start to gain weight.
