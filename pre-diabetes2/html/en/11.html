What does a healthy meal look like?

Now that you are familiar with the three major nutrients, let’s move onto how to create a healthy meal. When we we get hungry, we think about what food we are going to eat, not which nutrients (carbs, protein and fat) we’re going to have. For that reason, we have easy-to-follow guidelines to have a meal that’s healthy and balanced of all the major nutrients. You can use this method to plan your portions at every meal, so you won’t have to worry about calorie counting.
