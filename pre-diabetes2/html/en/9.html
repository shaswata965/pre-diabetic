Protein: Muscle Builder

Protein is an essential nutrient in any healthy diet and is one of the three main energy providing macronutrients, along with carbohydrates and fats. It helps the body to build, repair, and maintain most of your body's tissues and organs.

Protein can be found in both animal and plant foods, such as:

Meat

Poultry

Seafood

Beans

Peas

Eggs

Processed soy products

Nuts and seeds
