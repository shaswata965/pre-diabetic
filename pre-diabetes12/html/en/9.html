Stay Away

The two types of unhealthy fats that can harm your heart are: saturated fat and trans fat. Keep this in mind: any of these foods consumed occasionally and in small portions will not do immediate harm to your heart! Just make sure to completely stay away from trans fats by checking your food labels and leave your saturated fat intake for special occasions.

Foods high in saturated fat include:

  • Meats: red meat (ground beef, bologna), processed meats (hot dogs, sausage, beef bacon), Chicken skin
  • Dairy:High-fat dairy products, such as whole or 2% milk, cream, Full-fat labneh, ice cream, and full-fat cheese
  • Chocolate
  • Oils: ghee, butter, coconut oil

Foods high in trans fat include:

Processed foods made with hydrogenated oil or partially hydrogenated oil, such as donuts, cookies, chips, cakes.
