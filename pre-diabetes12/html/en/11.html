Prevention First

There are many things in your control that you can do to prevent heart disease. Make these 7 tips as part of your lifestyle, and not just a quick fix, for long-lasting benefits:

Eat a healthy diet

Eat vegetables, fruit and have at least two portions of fish, each week. Stick to unrefined, fiber-rich whole-grain foods and limit your sodium intake!

Get active

At least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity daily for five days each week

Control cholesterol

Eat healthy foods that are low in cholesterol, trans fats, and saturated fats, and high in fiber. Schedule a cholesterol screening with your doctor
