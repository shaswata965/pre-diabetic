Watch Out for Sodium

Eating foods that are high in sodium, also known as salt, can put you at risk for developing high blood pressure. If you regularly are eating foods that are packaged, not homemade, you may be consuming a lot of sodium without even knowing it! Foods that are high in sodium are: pastries with salty cheese, such as fatayer, salted laban (laban ayran), processed cheese, makdoos and pickled vegetables, soy sauce and ketchup.

But these aren’t the only foods you can find high levels of sodium in – sodium can be added to sweet foods as well! That’s why it’s important to always read the food label and to know what’s in the food you’re eating. When you cook your own meals, you have more control over how much salt to add, so that’s always the best option.
