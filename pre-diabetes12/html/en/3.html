Risk Factors

Studies have shown us that there are factors that can increase your risk for developing heart disease. Some of these factors you donâ€™t have control over. Males are at higher risk for heart disease and other genetic factors can place you at a higher risk. But, there are many more risk factors for heart disease that you DO have control over, such as:

  1. Smoking
  2. Overweight or obese
  3. Physical inactivity
  4. High blood pressure
  5. High blood cholesterol
  6. Diabetes

The good news is that these risk factors can be controlled by eating a well-balanced diet, staying physically active and avoiding smoking.
