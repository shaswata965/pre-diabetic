Find the Right Fats

Unhealthy fats like saturated and trans fats have been shown to increase your risk for heart disease if consumed regularly. You must eliminate all trans fats and limit your intake of saturated fats. Youâ€™re much better off sticking with the healthy fats, like monounsaturated fats, polyunsaturated fats and omega-3 fatty acids... but you should still limit your healthy fats as they are high in calories.

Foods high in monounsaturated fat:

  • Avocado
  • Canola oil
  • Almonds
  • Cashews
  • Pecans
  • Peanuts
  • Olives and olive oil
  • Peanut butter and peanut oil
  • Sesame seeds

Foods high in polyunsaturated fat:

  • Corn oil
  • Cottonseed oil
  • Oil-based salad dressings
  • CashewsPumpkin and sunflower seeds
  • Safflower oil
  • Soft (tub) margarine
  • Soybean oil
  • Sunflower oil
  • Walnuts

Foods high in Omega-3 fatty acids:

  • Albacore tuna
  • Herring
  • Rainbow trout
  • Salmon
  • Sardines
  • CashewsPumpkin and sunflower seeds
  • Walnuts
