Change your Thoughts from Negative to Positive

Switching your mindset from focusing on the negative thoughts to being positive isn’t always easy. Here are a few examples to show you how it’s done:


I can’t eat ice cream ever again


  • I can have ice cream once in a while.
  • When I do have ice cream, I’ll my portion size. That way, I won’t have too much.


Exercise is boring


  • I haven’t found an activity that I enjoy yet.
  • I’ll keep trying new activities until I find one that I like.


It’s too cold/hot outside to go for a walk


  • I will dress for the weather and walk anyway.
  • I will work out indoors


I can’t meet my eating goals because my family keeps making unhealthy treats


  • I will ask my family to make something healthy instead.


No one else supports my healthy lifestyle


  • My friend supports it.
  • I will ask for more support.


I haven’t stuck to my diet at all this week

  • I stuck to my Healthy eating plan four out of seven days.
  • I have a plan for how to stay on track in the future.
