Harmful and Helpful Thoughts

Developing the “right” mindset is important on your journey to healthy living. Harmful thoughts are negative and demotivating and get in the way of your eating and fitness goals, so they can help raise your risk for type 2 diabetes. Helpful thoughts can help you reach your eating and fitness goals and lower your risk for type 2 diabetes.

The three steps to deal with harmful thoughts are:

  • Write down when you have harmful thoughts and what they are. What thoughts get in the way of eating healthy and staying active?
  • Press the brakes. Some people like to picture a big red stop sign. How can you put the brakes on your harmful thoughts?
  • Think helpful thoughts can helpful thoughts can replace your harmful ones.
