Change your Thoughts

Here are a few more examples:


I’m too weak to start.


I can climb the stairs without getting out of breath now.

I’ll be a little more active each week.


I’m the world’s worst cook

My daughter liked the stir-fry I made last night.

I’ll keep learning more about cooking.


My friend has lost so much more weight than I have


My weight loss has slowed down. I’ll ask my friend for some tips.

Everyone loses weight at different rates and that’s okay.


My friend is so much stronger than I am


I’d like to be stronger. I’ll try using a resistance band.


I just know I’m going to get hurt. Then I won’t be able to work out


I’ll take steps to work out safely. If I do get hurt, I’ll find a different way to be active.


I just know I’m going to get type 2 diabetes, since both of my parents had it


I know a lot more about how to prevent type 2 diabetes than my parents did. I’ll do what I can to prevent type 2 diabetes
