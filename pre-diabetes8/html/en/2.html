Start with Stretching

Stretching is an important part of working out, as it prevents muscle soreness and improves flexibility!

How to stretch:

  • Do a short warm-up before stretching.

  • Move slowly until you feel the muscle stretch - A safe stretch is gentle and relaxing.

  • Hold the stretch steady for 15 to 30 seconds. Do not bounce.

  • Relax. Then repeat 3 to 5 times.

  • Stretch within your own limits. Do not push yourself.

  • Breathe slowly and naturally. Do not hold your breath

Droobi Tip

Listen to your body! If you feel pain in a muscle while stretching, stop right away!
