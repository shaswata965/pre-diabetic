Stop Exercising if…

You are experiencing chest pain. It may feel like a tightness or heaviness in the center of your chest, spreading throughout the chest or radiating from the chest to you shoulders and arms.

Immediately stop exercising - it the pain continues for another 2-4 minutes, go to the nearest hospital emergency room.

You have severe nausea, shortness of breath, sweating or feeling faint

Call your doctor. Your doctor can examine you to see what these symptoms are a sign of. Sweating is not usually a concern when you’re working out. However, if you are experiencing severe sweating in combination with any of the other symptoms mentioned above, seek immediate help from a doctor.
