Quiz
During Social Events

Allow yourself a small treat.

  • Have a healthy snack, like baby carrots or low fat yogurt before you go grocery shopping
  • Eat the healthy dish you bring. Ask how other dishes are prepared
  • Fill up on non-starchy veggies or a broth-based soup.
  • Keep a glass of water in your hand.
  • Say, “No, thank you.”
  • Stay away from the buffet.
  • Tell other guests about your eating plan
