The Menu Map

If the restaurant you go to has a lot of options, it may feel overwhelming to pick a dish. On menus, look for the words:

  • Baked
  • Broiled
  • Diet
  • Grilled
  • Healthy
  • Light
  • Low fat or fat-free
  • Steamed
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Whole grain or whole wheat
  • Food that is low in calories, fat and sugar, high in fiber and water; and high in vitamins, minerals, and protein.
