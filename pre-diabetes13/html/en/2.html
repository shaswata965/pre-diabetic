Challenges for Eating Well Out

It can be very challenging to stick to healthy choices when there’s so many tempting options around us. Here are some common problems that people face when eating out:

  • No healthy choices

  • Not knowing ingredients or how food was made

  • Tempting food

  • Wanting to be polite

  • Wanting to get your money’s worth

  • Wanting to join in

  • Pressure from others

  • Large portions
