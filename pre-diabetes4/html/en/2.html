MyPlate: Step-by-Step

In the previous lesson, we introduced how to build a healthy plate. Letâ€™s take a refresh and go through the guidelines:

On your dinner plate, imagine a line down the middle of the plate. Then on one half, imagine it being divided in half again, so you will have a total three sections on your plate.

Fill the largest section with non-starchy vegetables

Fill the one of the small sections with grains and starchy vegetables.

Fill the other small sections with proteins.

Add a serving of fruit, a serving of dairy or both as your meal plan allows.

Choose healthy fat in small amounts. For cooking, use oils. For salads, add some healthy additions such as nuts, seeds, avocado and vinaigrettes.

Add a low-calorie drink, or a no-calorie drink like water, unsweetened tea or coffee.

Now, we are going to talk about each of the food groups that are part of MyPlate: vegetables, grains, fruits, protein, and dairy.
