Protein

For proteins, stick to the leaner choices!

Very Lean:

Fish and Seafood - cod, hammour, flounder, haddock, halibut, or canned tuna in water
Tip: try to include fish at least 2 times per week

Poultry: chicken, turkey, or eggs.
Tip: choose poultry without the skin for less saturated fat and cholesterol

Lean:

Lean beef: round, flank, chuck, or rump roast

Medium to high fat:

Beef (ground beef, steak, prime rib, or corned beef)

Lamb

Veal cutlet

A healthier way to prepare meat is broiling, grilling, roasting, poaching or boiling. Fish that are caught locally are good sources of omega-3 fatty acids, like sardines, zobaidy (malabar cavalla) and gubgub (sea crab).

You can find protein, as well as vitamins and minerals, in plant-based foods like:

Beans (black, kidney, pinto)

Hummus and falafel

Peas

Edamame

Nuts and nut spreads

Tempeh

Tofu

Try switching your animal protein to include beans, legumes, peas and lentils for several meals per week.
