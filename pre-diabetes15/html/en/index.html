Lesson Overview

The people in your life who truly care about you will support your newly formed habits and lifestyle changes, because they want you to be happy. If they don’t know how to support you, it’s your responsibility to let them know how they can.

This week’s lesson will take you through:

Gaining support from groups, clubs and classes

There are many ways to find people with similar goals to yourself

Options for receiving support from professionals

Don’t think you’re in this alone - your healthcare team want you to succeed
