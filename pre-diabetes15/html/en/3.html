Support from Groups

Groups:

Start a group chat with all your friends who are also in the process of losing weight. Use it as a place to share with each other struggles you’ve faced along the way.

Join a group of friends and attend the farmers’ market together and shop for healthy ingredients together.

Join support groups either in person or online

Support groups are for people who share a common issue. For example, people who have pre-diabetes and want to lower their risk of type 2 diabetes and live a healthy lifestyle.

Support group members share facts, ideas, and feelings. They listen to and encourage each other.
