Other Challenges

I don’t have a car

  • Work out in your own home or area.

I don’t have child care

  • Be active with your family.
  • Ask other friends or family to help out.
  • Exercise in front of your kids –you’re setting a good example for your kids.
  • Use gym child care.

My area is not safe

  • Work out in your own home.
  • Work out at a gym or community center.

It costs too much

  • Do free activities like walking in the park.
  • Buy workout clothes and equipment on sale.
  • Look for free fitness classes at your local gym.
