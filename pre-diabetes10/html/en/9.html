Socialize and Energize

While there are some people who prefer to do their exercise alone, others may prefer the social engagement and will sign up for group classes. If being around other people encourages you to exercise, consider doing some or all of the following:

  • Join a walking club.
  • Take a fitness class.
  • Talk on the phone with a friend while you march in place, walk, or climb stairs.
  • Walk with a family member or friend.
  • Go out dancing with family or friend.
  • If you have young kids, push your child in a stroller.
  • Play soccer with a group of friends.
