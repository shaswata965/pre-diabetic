How to Make Time for Activity

To improve your chance of success, we advise you to plan ahead and prepare for any obstacles you may face.

If you struggle to find time to exercise, as many of us do, try these suggestions:

  • Break your 150 minutes into smaller chunks.
  • Park your car farther away from the place you want to go to.
  • Take stairs the instead of the elevator.
  • Stretch, do sit-ups, or pedal a stationary bike while you watch TV.
  • Plant and care for a vegetable or flower garden..
  • Take a brisk walk during your coffee or lunch break.
  • Ask a friend to go with you to take part in an exercise program at work or join a nearby gym
