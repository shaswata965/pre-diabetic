Ways to Reduce Stress

The next time you feel that overwhelmed and stress, try some of these tips to help you manage your stress in a healthy way:

  • Ask for help
    Don’t be scaredFeel free to ask your friends and family for help. They care about you and want the best for you.

  • Try Meditation
    Sit still, close your eyes and focus on each breath you take.

  • Get enough sleep
    Aim for 8 hours per night.

  • Journal
    Writing your feelings down is a healthy way to reflect and understand your situation.

  • Create tools to help yourself manage your day.
    Set small, doable goals. Have a to do list, set reminders.

  • Take care of your body and mind
    That way, you’ll be more prepared to tackle stressful situations. If you don’t take care of yourself you won’t be able to help yourself and others

  • Get moving!
    Do something active—even if it’s just a walk around the block.

  • Cut back on caffeine
    Caffeine can make you feel jumpy and anxious. Try an herbal tea, like mint or chamomile.

  • Soothe yourself
    Get a massage, take a hot bath, have a cup of herbal tea, or put on some calming music.

  • Do something fun
    Go out dancing, go shopping, call a friend. Do whatever you enjoy—as long as it’s healthy
