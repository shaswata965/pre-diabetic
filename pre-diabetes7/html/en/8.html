Relaxation Techniques

It is possible to cope with stress using the following relaxation techniques:

  • Relax your muscles. Scrunch up your face muscles. Hold for 5 seconds. Now release. Feel your face muscles relax. Do the same with your jaw, shoulder, arm, chest, leg, and foot muscles in turn

  • Imagine a scene that makes you feel peaceful. Try to picture yourself there.

  • Breathe deeply. Relax your stomach muscles. Place one hand just below your ribs. Take a slow, deep breath through your nose. Feel your hand go up. Now breathe out slowly through your mouth. Make sure to breathe out all the way. Feel your hand go down.
