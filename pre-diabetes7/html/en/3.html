Khalid’s Story

Khalid is a 27-year old engineer. He was recently promoted at his job and now feels overwhelmed on a daily basis, because of all the responsibility and pressure, long working hours and tight deadlines. But all this stress has negatively affected his daily habits – Khalid regularly skips breakfast and instead grabs a pastry from a coffee shop. He doesn’t think it’s important to exercise, because of all his responsibilities. After a long day at work and skipping lunch, he copes with his stress by eating a very large, unhealthy meal while checking his social media. At a doctor’s appointment, Khalid was informed by his doctor that he is at high-risk for developing Type 2 diabetes.

Stress does not always bring the best out of us. We may deal with stress by:

Eating unhealthy foods

Skipping workouts

Watching too much TV or spending too much time looking at screens

Smoking
