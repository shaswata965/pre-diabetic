Better Eating Habits

How you eat is just as important as what you eat. Here are some eating habits we recommend you practice:

  • Set up a regular pattern of eating. Do your best to avoid having one large meal a day. Spread your food out evenly across the whole day. This helps you to avoid getting too hungry and overeating later on. A good rule is 3 meals with 1-2 healthy snacks.

  • Eat slowly. Some people refer to this as mindful eating. Eating at a slower pace (over 20 minutes) allows you to enjoy your food, digest better and will help you pick up on when your body is full. When food is consumed too fast, you miss out on your body’s signals.
  • Change your eating environment. Avoid eating while watching TV or looking at a screen. Distractions while you’re eating will take your attention away from your meal and prevent you from enjoying and appreciating your meal from start to finish
