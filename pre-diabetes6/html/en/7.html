Healthy Snacks to-go!

Try these quick, easy and healthy snacks if you’re ever in a crunch for time:

  1. Handful nuts(4 half walnuts or 6 medium size cashew or almonds)
  2. Yogurt low fat with cucumber
  3. Yogurt low fat with fruits (strawberry, blueberry and mango)
  4. Milk with 3 pieces of biscuits or 1 1/2 shaboora or ½ cup corn flakes
  5. Smoothies(low fat milk + ½ cup unsweetened frozen fruits)
  6. Sauteed vegetables with olive oil and oregano
  7. Popcorn(homemade unsalted, no butter or caramel)
  8. Oatmeal Pudding(low fat milk + 2 tablespoon oatmeal + one teaspoon sweetener)
