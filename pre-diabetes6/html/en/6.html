Healthy Meals to-go!

Are you usually in a rush? Try these quick, easy and healthy meals that can save you time:

  1. Salad (tomato, cucumber, colored pepper, lettuce, root beet, cube of low fat cheese, 2 tablespoon of kidney beans, 2 tablespoon corn or you can add any preferred vegetables).
  2. Soup (carrot, mushroom, squash, broccoli, onion, chicken cube, 3 tablespoon oatmeal).
  3. Lentil soup with vegetable.
  4. Egg sandwich egg,tomato,onion,parsley,whole-wheat, 2 slice of bread).
  5. Tuna salad.
  6. Baked vegetable (mushroom, onion, broccoli, one medium size potato) with grilled chicken or fish
  7. Pasta salad (cherry tomato, olives, mushroom, capsicum, low-fat white cheese).
