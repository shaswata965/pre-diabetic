Healthy Meals to-go!

Are you usually in a rush? Try these quick, easy and healthy meals that can save you time:

Salad (tomato, cucumber, colored pepper, lettuce, root beet, cube of low fat cheese, 2 tablespoon of kidney beans, 2 tablespoon corn or you can add any preferred vegetables).

Soup (carrot, mushroom, squash, broccoli, onion, chicken cube, 3 tablespoon oatmeal).

Lentil soup with vegetable.

Egg sandwich egg,tomato,onion,parsley,whole-wheat, 2 slice of bread).

Tuna salad.

Baked vegetable (mushroom, onion, broccoli, one medium size potato) with grilled chicken or fish

Pasta salad (cherry tomato, olives, mushroom, capsicum, low-fat white cheese).
