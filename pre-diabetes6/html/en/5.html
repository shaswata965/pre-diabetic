Smart Substitutions

When you’re preparing meals, try these healthy food swaps to increase the nutrition of your meal and reduce the number of calories you’re consuming.

For Vegetables: Instead of fried vegetables, try grilling or sautéing in olive oil. Instead of mashed potatoes, try a mixture of mashed cauliflower and potato (it will have a similar texture to mashed potatoes!)

For Protein: Instead of fried and breaded meat, chicken and fish try grilling, boiling, baking, or using an air-fryer. Add spices and herbs for added flavor.

For Grains: Instead of granola, try Oatmeal cooked in water and low-fat milk. Instead of white rice, try Brown Rice, Quinoa, or Bulgur. Instead of pasta, try zucchini cut with a spiralizer.

For Dairy: Instead of full-fat milk and sweetened condensed milk, try low-fat milk. Instead of full-fat cooking cream and sour cream, try Greek yogurt.

For Fats: Instead of mayonnaise, try hummus or avocado
Instead of butter, try olive oil or avocado oil.
