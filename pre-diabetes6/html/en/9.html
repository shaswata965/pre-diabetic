Challenges with Food Shopping

Preparing to cook a health meal requires food shopping! Here are some solutions for common challenges people face to buy healthy food:

Challenge: Shopping for and cooking healthy meals this way takes up too much time.

Ways to cope:
  • Plan ahead. Spend some time in the weekend to plan menu items for the following week. Look for recipes for fast, healthy meals. Plan a few menu items that can be cooked once, but served twice.
