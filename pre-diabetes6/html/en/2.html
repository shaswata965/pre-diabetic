Serving Size

A serving is a specific amount of food with a known calorie and nutrient count. Plan your portion and serving sizes along with the choice of ingredients to make a healthy meal ahead of time.

Liquid and soft foods can be measured using a measuring cup or spoon.

Fruits and Vegetables can be measured depending on how you prepare them. If you eat a whole fruit or vegetable, it is measured by the size of the piece. If you chop it up, it is measured in cups.

Meat can be measured in ounces or grams with a food scale.

Plan your portions sizes along with the choice of ingredients to make a healthy meal ahead of time.
