How to Overcome Bad Triggers

Here’s how to deal with triggers in the space around you that spark unhealthy eating:

  • Stay away from the cue or keep it out of sight.
  • Build a new, healthier habit.
    • Add a new cue that helps you lead a healthier life.
    • Practice reacting to the cue in a healthier way to form a habit.

Droobi Tip

The next time you go to the grocery store, do not leave the house on an empty stomach. This will tempt you to buy snack you don’t need. Also, avoid the sections of the store that are tempting for you (such as the chocolate aisle). Just stick to items on your grocery list!.
