What is pre-diabetes?

Pre-diabetes means that your blood sugar is higher than normal. But it’s not high enough for you to be diagnosed with type 2 diabetes.

Prediabetes is a reversible condition and this program can help you reduce your chance of developing type 2 diabetes and its complications.

If you are prediabetic, you are more likely to get:

  • Diabetes
  • Heart disease
  • Stroke

→ Did you know…?

Did you know that people with prediabetes are 5 to 15 times more likely to develop type 2 diabetes and are at higher risk for conditions like heart disease and stroke?
