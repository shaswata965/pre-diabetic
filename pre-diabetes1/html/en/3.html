Prevention

Why is it important to lose weight and be more active?

Losing weight can help you to:

  • Prevent or delay type 2 diabetes
  • Lower blood pressure
  • Keep cholesterol in healthy ranges
  • Increase self-confidence

Being active can also help you to prevent diabetes. Here are a list of some of the benefits of being active:

  • Burn Calories
  • Feel more energetic
  • Look and feel better
  • Sleep better
  • Higher energy levels
  • Lower blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Increased muscle and bone strength
  • Improved memory
