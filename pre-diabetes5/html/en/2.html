Let’s Get Started

Over the next few weeks, your physical activity target will slowly increase so that you will be doing at least 150 minutes of physical activity every week. Before we learn more about how to stay active, it’s important that you think about the following:

  • How active am I now and how active was I in the past?
  • What do I like and dislike about being active or inactive?
  • If I have been inactive in the past, what was stopping me?

Answering these questions will help you decide what you want to do and how much physical activity is best for you.
