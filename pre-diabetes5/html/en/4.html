Introduction to Physical Activity

Making physical activity part of your daily lifestyle is part of the equation for weight loss. Physical Activity is any activity that gets you moving and helps you burn energy. It can include the time you spend walking from your car to the office, climbing a flight of stairs, or carrying groceries into your home.

Exercise generally refers to a specific, planned physical activity such as swimming, biking, or running. Please keep in mind that you do not need to be an athlete to be capable of doing exercise. All exercise means is physical activity that it is a structured part of your day, like going to a dance class right after work or swimming in the mornings.

If you’re at the beginning of your physically active lifestyle, remember that any physical activity is better than none at all. Staying active as part of your daily lifestyle burns calories even if it’s not part of a structured activity plan. Take small steps to add more movement into your daily lifestyle.
