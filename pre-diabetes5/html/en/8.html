Create Your Plan

A physical activity plan should answer the following questions

  • What exercise programs or activities are safe & practical for you to do regularly?
  • Where will you do these activities and at what time of day will you do your program?
  • How often will you exercise, and how long will you exercise each time?

Please remember to consult your doctor before setting up your physical activity plan.

Droobi Tip

It is important to select the activities you enjoy to ensure you follow it throughout your physical activity plan and stay motivated.
