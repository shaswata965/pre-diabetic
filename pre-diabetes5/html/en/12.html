F.I.T.T - Time

Your goal is to stay active for at least 10 minutes at a time. Slowly increase to 20 minutes or more at a time. You want to continue to increase your activity time slowly.

Droobi has already calculated your daily and weekly activity targets. Droobi will gradually increase your activity targets so you can reach your goal.

Studies show that there are still health benefits if you break up your activity into three 10-minute sessions. When it comes to controlling blood glucose levels, doing three 10-minute sessions may be just as effective as or even better than doing 30 minutes all at once.
