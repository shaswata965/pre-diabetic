Benefits of Physical Activity

Increasing your level of physical activity is one of the best ways to prevent your risk of chronic diseases like type 2 diabetes and heart disease. There are a number of other benefits that include:

  • Improves blood pressure and reduces belly fat

  • Strengthens your heart and lowers your risk for heart disease


  • Reduce your “bad” LDL cholesterol levels and increase your HDL “good” cholesterol

  • Lowers stress and anxiety levels, reduces depression


  • Burns calories to help you lose or maintain weight

  • Increases your energy for daily activities

  • Helps you sleep better

  • Strengthens your muscles and bones and keeps your joints flexible
