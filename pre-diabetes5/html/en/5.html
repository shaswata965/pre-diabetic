Activity is Part of the Solution

“You can’t outrun a healthy diet!” While exercise is important for your weight loss, exercise alone is not as effective as a healthy diet.

One of the reasons being is that it’s very easy to consume the same number of calories that were burned during exercise, because we tend to feel hungrier after a workout. You can spend half an hour and burn 200 calories, but replace those calories by eating an extra protein bar right after.

But exercise has benefits that go beyond just weight loss… You can experience a change in your body composition, which is the percentage of fat and muscle your body has.

Exercise should NOT be ignored. Your exercise plan should include moderate-intensity aerobic exercise. Regular physical activity can also help maintain muscle mass while promoting fat loss.
