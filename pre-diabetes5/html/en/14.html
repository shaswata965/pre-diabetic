Ways to Get Active

Try some of these strategies to get started. The first step is the hardest, but most important to starting your journey for weight

  • Fast walking inside malls is a great way to reach your goal step count if itâ€™s too hot outside
  • Pace the sidelines at sports events
  • Play actively with your family or pets for 15 to 30 minutes a day
  • Replace relaxing drives with relaxing walks
  • Break up your sitting time in the office by walking to talk to your co-workers instead of sending them emails
  • Start a new active hobby, such as biking, hiking or dragon boating
  • Take a walk after dinner with your family or by yourself
  • Walk around whenever you talk on the phone
  • Walk briskly when you shop
  • Walk up and down escalators instead of just riding them
  • When you watch TV, stand up and move during the ads, or do chores
